Food generated from plants or animals whose DNA has been transformed through genetic engineering is referred to as genetically modified food (or GM food). These GMOs are short for genetically modified organisms.The practise of directly modifying an organism's genes, such as by introducing DNA from different species, is known as genetic engineering.
